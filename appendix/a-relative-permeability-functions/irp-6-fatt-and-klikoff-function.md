# IRP=6  Fatt and Klikoff function

_IRP_ = 6           Functions of Fatt and Klikoff (1959)

$${k_{rl}}\quad  = \quad {\left( {{S^*}} \right)^3}$$

$${k_{rg}}\quad = \quad {\left( {1; - ;{S^*}} \right)^3}$$

$${S^*}\quad  = \quad \frac{{\left( {{S_l}\; - \;{S_{lr}}} \right)}}{{\left( {1\; - \;{S_{lr}}} \right)}}$$

with Slr = _RP(1)_.
